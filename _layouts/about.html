    In 2008, I started to work as freelance translator, reviewer, proofreader, and Language Quality Assessment (LQA) specialist in these language pairs: Portuguese(pt-br), English(uk-us), French, and Spanish to Italian.

Thanks to these opportunities, I had the chance to build up a professional knowledge in the fields of logistics, finance and accounting - which are now part of my expertise in technical translation and reviewing.

    Here is quick information about my educational background:

    In 2007, I took my Master’s Degree in Conference Interpreting and Translation (Italian, French, Spanish) at the university of Trieste.

    When I moved to Portugal, in 2009, I studied Portuguese Language and Culture at the University of Coimbra, then passed the DAPLE exam of Instituto Camões in Lisbon.

    I also decided to specialize in Business French taking the Diplôme supérieur de français commercial in the Chamber of Commerce of Paris. Well, actually I’ve never stopped looking for opportunities to improve and acquire new professional language skills - so now I’m attending a Specialist Master's Programme in Translation for Publishing from English to Italian in Turin.
